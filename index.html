Are you, or someone you care about, experiencing problems with gambling?

Problem gambling can be treated and people do recover.

The MA Problem Gambling Helpline is the official problem gambling helpline of the MA Department of Public Health. We offer referrals to problem gambling treatment services and support resources. MA Problem Gambling Helpline services are free and confidential.

Trained Specialists are available to help 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
